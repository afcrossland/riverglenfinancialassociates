Portfolio review service

A full, independent review of your existing portfolio — with a dedicated point of contact and personalised guidance.

Our portfolio review service gives you access to a dedicated adviser who will provide a thorough, independent assessment of your current investment portfolio and work with you on strategy, fund selection and communication — as often as suits you.

To arrange an initial meeting, please contact Nigel Lindley on 01778 421122.

What's included

  • A detailed portfolio report
  • Full review of your fund portfolio
  • Assessment of new funds and removal of underperforming assets where appropriate
  • Risk attitude verification to ensure your investments remain suitable
  • Confirmation of your personal circumstances and any changes since the last review
  • Permission reaffirmation for any recommended changes
The value of your investment can go down as well as up and you may not get back the full amount invested. Past performance is not a guide to future performance.
