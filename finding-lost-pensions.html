Our services

Finding lost pensions & pension consolidation

Helping you track down old pension pots — and bring them together — at no initial cost to you.

If you've worked for multiple employers over the years, there's a good chance you have pension pots you've lost track of. This is far more common than most people realise — and the sums involved can be significant.

At Riverglen, locating your lost pensions is a complimentary service, provided entirely at our expense. We use specialist tools to trace pensions from previous employers, private schemes and public sector arrangements. There is no obligation to take any further action once we've located them.

How it works

  • Get in touch with a brief summary of your employment history and any pension details you have
  • We use a specialist tracing service to locate any pensions linked to your name
  • We present you with what we find and explain your options clearly
  • If you'd like advice on consolidating or optimising your pensions, we'll outline our fees openly before any further work begins
