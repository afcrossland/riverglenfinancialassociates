Care fees planning

Clear, compassionate advice on one of life's most complex — and often unexpected — financial decisions.

Planning for the costs of care — whether for yourself or a family member — can be one of the most challenging financial conversations to have. The rules are complex, the costs are significant, and decisions often need to be made quickly and under pressure.

At Riverglen, we're experienced in helping individuals and families navigate this area, finding straightforward solutions to what can be a daunting financial situation. We approach care fees planning with sensitivity, taking the time to understand your personal circumstances before providing clear, practical advice.

What we can help with

  • Assessing current and future care needs and their likely costs
  • Identifying funding shortfalls and finding solutions to bridge them
  • Advising on state benefits and entitlements you may not be aware of
  • Guidance on estate planning, wills and lasting powers of attorney (LPAs)
  • Inheritance tax planning to preserve wealth for future generations
The Financial Conduct Authority does not regulate taxation & trust advice and will writing.
