Our services

Retirement planning

Professional advice at any age to help ensure your retirement is everything you want it to be.

Retirement represents a major change in lifestyle — and gives you a crucial new asset: time. The decisions you make in the years approaching retirement can have a profound impact on the quality of life you enjoy, and professional advice can make a significant difference.

Whether you're just starting to think about retirement or are ready to begin drawing an income, Riverglen can help you build a comprehensive plan. We go beyond pensions to consider all the income sources and assets available to you — integrating savings, investments, property and more.

What we can help with

  • Review and consolidation of personal and workplace pensions
  • Setting up or reviewing a workplace pension scheme
  • Integrating savings, investments and other assets into your retirement plan
  • Cash flow modelling to project your retirement income
  • Retirement budget planning
  • Tax-efficient income withdrawal strategies
  • Lifetime allowance planning
  • Inheritance and legacy planning
The value of your investment and any income from it can go down as well as up and you may not get back the full amount invested. Tax treatment depends on individual circumstances and may be subject to change.
