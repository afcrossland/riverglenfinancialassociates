Protection

The right cover in place for you, your family and your business — reviewed as your circumstances change.

Our protection needs evolve throughout our lives — from taking on a mortgage or starting a family, through to supporting a business or planning for retirement. It's important to review your arrangements regularly to make sure they remain right for you.

At Riverglen, we advise on a wide range of protection products and take the time to understand your situation before making any recommendation. Whether you need personal cover, family protection or business continuity planning, we can help.

Products we advise on

  • Income protection (PHI) — Replaces a proportion of your income if you're unable to work due to illness, accident or disability. Particularly valuable for the self-employed, who may not receive sick pay from an employer.
  • Critical illness insurance — Pays a lump sum on diagnosis of a specified critical condition. Most policies cover heart attack, stroke, cancer and MS as a minimum, with many extending to 30–40 additional conditions.
  • Family income benefit — Provides a regular, ongoing income to your family rather than a lump sum, helping to replace the income they would lose on your death or diagnosis of a critical illness.
  • Accident, sickness & unemployment (ASU) — Short-term income protection covering up to 12 months on a valid claim. A useful safety net, particularly alongside a mortgage.
  • Private medical insurance — Access to private treatment when you need it. Options include add-ons such as dental and optical cover.
