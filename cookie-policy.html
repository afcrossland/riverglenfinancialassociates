Cookie policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or actions. This website uses cookies to help us understand how visitors use the site and to improve your experience.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies — These are necessary for the website to function correctly. They enable basic navigation and access to secure areas. The website cannot operate properly without these cookies.
  • Performance cookies — These help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to identify areas for improvement and measure how well the site is working.
  • Preference cookies — These remember your choices and settings, such as language or regional preferences, so you don't have to re-enter them each time you visit.
  • Statistics cookies — These collect anonymous data about how visitors use the site, including pages visited and time spent on each page. This information is used to improve the website.
  • Marketing cookies — These track your browsing activity across websites to enable us and our partners to show you more relevant advertising. You can opt out of these through your browser settings.

Third-party links

Our website may contain links to external sites with their own cookie and privacy policies that are outside our control. We recommend reviewing those policies independently when visiting third-party websites.

How to control and delete cookies

You can manage, block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. The exact steps vary by browser — look for the Privacy or Settings section in:

  • Google Chrome — Settings › Privacy and security › Cookies and other site data
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings › Cookies and site permissions
  • Mozilla Firefox — Options › Privacy & Security
  • Apple Safari — Preferences › Privacy
  • Opera — Settings › Advanced › Privacy & security

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.
